Control your expenses!

Swile ( lunchr ) is an application that simplifies the management of luncheon vouchers by companies. The company provides employees with a Mastercard connected to an application. Each month, the Lunchr account is funded by the company. However, at the moment, it is complicated to keep track of lunchr activities because the money provided by the company is not directly accessible and visible on the bank account.
For each activity, the user should be informed directly to allow him to manage his expenses. This will save time, he will be informed in real time of the status of his Lunchr account.

In order to control our spending, we could create an applet tha would send a text to the user according to his activities. For instance, when the user approaches 0 in his lunchr account, a text could warn him " Careful! You have only 20 euros left on your account". We could think of other scenarios such as sending a sms when the user spends more than a particular amount ( define the amount).

How will it work ?

  • Register and create your account and click on” new applet”
  • Click on"+this" to select the trigger
  • Search and select webhook
  • Click on the connect button
  • Click on "receive a request"
  • Name your event "lunchr activities"and click on "create trigger"
  • Click on "+that"to select the action to accomplish
  • Search and select "sms"and choose "send a sms"
  • Enter the phone Number
  • click on "create action" and "finish"
    • you will then have to code the event that will action the webhook
